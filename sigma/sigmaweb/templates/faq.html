sigma hackathon planner FAQ

Anyone interested in spreading their passion for STEM to middle schoolers! We provide potential design challenges, workshop ideas, and template schedule plans for the hackathon, so follow the guide.

Who can do this?

Anyone interested in spreading their passion for STEM to middle schoolers! We provide potential design challenges, workshop ideas, and template schedule plans for the hackathon, so follow the guide.


How long would this event take?

Our template schedule has the hackathon for about 4 to 5 hors. However, feel free to make it shorter or longer if you would like.


What should we do about obtaining parental consent to work with the students?

We will be providing more information about working with middle schools, safety, and parental consent forms. This is all under Organizer resources.


What about SWAG? How can I get food/t-shirts for the middle schoolers?

One way is to get funding from your school. More information under Organizer Resources/Budgeting. Going to restaurants and asking about food sponsors are also potential solutions.
